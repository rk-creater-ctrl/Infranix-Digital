Service Policy

How CodeNexus handles project inquiries, delivery, revisions, and support.

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Project Inquiry

Clients can request services through WhatsApp, the order form, email, or contact page. The first discussion is used to understand requirements, deadline, budget, and expected deliverables.

Scope Confirmation

Before work begins, CodeNexus confirms the service type, pages or features, content requirements, timeline, and revision count. Any major feature added later may require a revised timeline or quote.

Delivery

Delivery may include website files, live GitHub Pages links, software files, screenshots, documentation, or setup guidance depending on the project. Delivery timelines are estimates and depend on project complexity and client response time.

Revisions

Revisions cover reasonable changes within the agreed scope. New features, major redesigns, or extra modules are treated as additional work.

Support

Basic handover support is provided after delivery. Long-term maintenance, new changes, hosting support, or feature updates can be discussed separately.

No Online Checkout

This website currently handles service inquiries only. Orders are discussed directly with CodeNexus before any project is confirmed.
